About

Gaurav Hrishikesh Thakur is a Film Director, Writer, and Editor based in Bremen, Germany. Creatively introspective and utopian in vision, he has spent over a decade crafting independent films and commercial projects that fuse high-end visual storytelling with deeply personal narratives.

From the age of 14, Gaurav knew he wanted to be a filmmaker — one who could integrate breathtaking visual effects into emotionally resonant stories. His father first opened his eyes to the art of cinematic construction, and that early curiosity grew into a lifelong craft.

He founded his own production company Comutador Junkies Production in 2012, and has since independently directed, written, produced, and edited over 20 film projects — including music videos, documentaries, and commercial work for the Indian Army. His short film Era of Light received an Official Selection at the Indian National Awards.

Currently, Gaurav is developing his debut feature film — a project supported by two years of on-field research with the Indian Armed Forces.
